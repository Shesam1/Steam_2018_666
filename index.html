At Your Service

On-Site

Choose from a selection of on-site services that free you to enjoy your Manhattan home. Other services are available on request. Let’s talk and see how Gate Keeper NY can assist you.

  • Apartment visits to inspect for well-being and irregularities due to building activity, regular wear and tear, weather conditions and owner concerns
  • Emergency repairs, manage apartment upgrades and improvements incorporating building standards, and requirements into all projects
  • Meet with contractors and vendors to plan projects and secure bids
  • Schedule and oversee internet/cable service installation and maintenance
  • Test security systems
  • Coordination of housekeeping, periodic window, floor and other maintenance treatments
  • Run all faucets and toilets regularly, water plants, consultation and purchasing assistance for furnishings, floor coverings, window treatments and other apartment contents

Operational Services

Gate Keeper NY also works behind the scene and will set up a custom plan designed specifically for you. We offer a selection of off-site services that provide comfort and assistance assuring owners that their home is being cared for.

  • Account set up and invoice payments covering monthly maintenance or common charges, internet/cable fees, utilities and other apartment expenses
  • Record keeping of invoices, quotes and project summaries
  • Regular apartment activity reports
  • Mail forwarding
  • Incoming and outgoing package shipments
  • Building management liaison
